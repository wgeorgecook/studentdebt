President Biden partially fulfilled his campaign promise to forgive at minimum $10,000 for each American with student debt by limiting who qualifies for forgiveness.

With the repayment deadline looming, President Biden announced student loan forgiveness for borrowers making up to $120k. This order forgives $10k for single people, and $20k for Pell Grant recipients. Loan balances remaining will start repayment in January 2023.
